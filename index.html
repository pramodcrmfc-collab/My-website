About

I spend my time at the intersection of complex operational logic, changing global regulations, and enterprise technology.

Over the past 11 years, my role hasn't just been about implementing software — it's been about acting as the bridge that translates messy, intricate business requirements into seamless, scalable digital platforms. Whether it was mapping out 100+ distinct government services and customised SLAs for UAE Freezones using MS Dynamics, or defining the functional requirements for a custom booking engine that integrated with global travel aggregators like Amadeus and Sabre, my focus has always been the same: making the complex simple for the end-user.

I currently serve as the Solution Lead for our global E-Invoicing platform, navigating rigorous regulatory frameworks across multiple countries — including KSA (ZATCA), the UAE (FTA), Malaysia, Oman, Cambodia, Singapore, Japan, Australia and New Zealand. From spearheading initial gap assessments to managing successful go-live for dozens of clients in the KSA region, I ensure strict regulatory compliance integrates seamlessly with smooth, uninterrupted business operations.

I'm a firm believer that modern delivery requires modern execution. I actively integrate generative AI into my daily workflows to scale operations and eliminate traditional bottlenecks — using tools like Claude to architect multi-country data dictionaries, and Gemini & NotebookLM to automate hyper-accurate, documentation-backed client support.

Open to connecting with people in e-invoicing & compliance tech, CRM implementation, or anyone using AI tools seriously in delivery work.
